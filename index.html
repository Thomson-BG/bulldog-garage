From Our Shop to the Industry

Bulldogs get hired

Program graduates have gone on to college automotive programs, dealership service departments, and independent shops across the valley. Internship partners put students in real bays before they ever frame a diploma — and some never leave.

Former Bulldog Garage students have continued into Mt. San Jacinto College's automotive program and earned internships at local shops like Idyllwild Garage — turning class periods into careers.— Program highlights
Your Instructor

Mr. Joshua Thomson

★ ASE Certified Master Technician

The Bulldog Garage runs the way a professional shop runs — with standards, sign-offs, and pride in the work. Mr. Thomson brings master-level industry certification to the classroom and the shop floor, training students on the same diagnostics, equipment, and expectations they'll meet in the field.
